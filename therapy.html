Emotional Exercises

Deep Breathing Exercise

Helps calm the nerves and promotes inner peace. Sit in a quiet place, inhale deeply for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 6 seconds. Repeat for 5-10 minutes.

Physical Activity Exercise

Boosts mood and increases energy. Take a 20-minute walk, do yoga, or stretch while listening to calming or upbeat music.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR)

Relieves muscle tension and promotes deep relaxation. Tense each muscle group from your feet to your head for 5-10 seconds, then release the tension and focus on the feeling of relaxation.

Gratitude Exercise

Helps cultivate positive thinking and gratitude. Write down three things you're grateful for each day, big or small, and focus on the positive aspects of your life.

Emotion Awareness Exercise

Enhances emotional awareness and regulation. Identify the emotions you're experiencing, where you feel them in your body, and acknowledge them without judgment. Try to transform negative emotions into positive ones.

Self-Acceptance Exercise

Promotes self-acceptance and self-compassion. Reflect on and embrace a part of yourself that you might find challenging. Celebrate your positive qualities and their impact on your life.
